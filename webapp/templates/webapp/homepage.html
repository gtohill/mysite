Our Philosophy. Student Centred Learning.

Every kid is unique. Every kid has the potential to do great things. Some kids learn faster than others. To accommodate for different learning styles, our ratio of student to instructor is 4:1. Our small class size ensures that each student learns at a pace that is challenging and fun. Our commitment is to help your child get one step closer to achieving his/her potential.

Experiential Learning. Deeper Insight. Long-Lasting Learning

"for the things we have to learn before we can do them, we learn by doing them." ~ Aristotle

Each class is designed to focus on a core computing concept. Our instructors demonstrates the concept with a real programming example. The student is encouraged to reflect and internalize the demonstration. After sufficient repetition the student creates a blue print in their mind about the concept. To ensure student understands the concept, our instructors guide the each student as he/she implements the concept in a real programming environment.

Create In Code. My Story.

In my younger years, I LOVED video games. In 1999, I was the Playadium Video Game Champion. 20 years later, I now enjoy teaching young people the benefits of coding.

Learning to code is the new 4th R to education. What better way to learn how to code then through video games.

I have separate degrees in Computer Science and Philosophy from the University of Western Ontario. I have over 20 years experience as an entrepreneur in the technology sector. Most of my experience is centered around developing technology to improve human capital acquisition. After years of seeing a dramatic shortage between the demand for skilled programmers and the supply, I decided to focus on helping kids find opportunities to explore their interest in learning to code.

My commitment to you: I will give you my best every day. I will take care to ensure you are gaining value from our courses. I will create an environment that is fun, collaborative, and builds confidence. You can reach me at gtohill@createincode.com
