Novice

Our Novice course is ideal for students who are ages 12-15 or who have completed the Beginner course. Students must have good typing skills, basic understanding of variable assignment, conditional loops, and conditional (if/then) statements.

In this course, students will learn the foundations of computer game design, then create a video game. Students will learning to implement programming concepts, such as basic algorithms, classes, functions, methods, and arrays into their game.

Intermediate

This course puts the "Science" in Computer Science. It will introduce students to the core concepts taught during the first year of university

Our intermediate course is designed for older students who want to apply more advanced mathematical concepts while learning pre-University computer science algorithms. Students will create a more complex and efficient video game using advanced mathematics, object oriented design, and basic data structures. This course is perfect for high school students interested pursuing a minor, major, or specialization Degree in Computer Science.

The development environment for this course is Python or Java.
