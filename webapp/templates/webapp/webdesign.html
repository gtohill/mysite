With all the website template companies (like WIX), why learn website design yourself?

Using a web template is a great idea for basic, static, websites. However, if you want to add custom features and dynamic content to your site, then you'll need to how to create, design and implement that content. Our web site design course will teach you these critical development skills.

In our fast-paced and competitive world, the ability to quickly deploy and update web content such as a blog, personal branding, advertising a product/service, offers those with limited resources the opportunity to compete against anyone.

Guided by our instructors, the focus of this 18 week course is to teach students to use JavaScript to create a dynamic website. Students will learn the basics of HTML5 and CSS3. Basic HTML5 and CSS3 templates are provided during the actual development of a website.
